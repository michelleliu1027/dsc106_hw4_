McDonald's Sales Report

Monthly Data Analysis




By looking at the line chart of monthly total sales of those 5 products at the 5 locations you provided, what we find first is that, in general, from 2016 to 2019, North East region is where our products are consumed by customers the most while the Central region is where our products are least consumed by customers. Therefore, in order to expand our profits, we should devote our energies more into the North East region to win more favors from them.

Also, one alerting thing from the above plot is that, in all those locations, after October 2018 which is exactly the time when Burger King introduced the Impossible Burger, the sales of our products seems to decrease in an immediate speed but not lasting. To justify this kind of view, let's see another graph.




The bar chart above indicates that after the introduction of Impossible Burger from Burger King (Oct 2018), the sales of McDonald in all 5 regions occurred a shocking decrement. However, such decrement did not last for a long time: in the later months (Nov 2018 â€” Sep 2019), we can see that the sales varied a little bit. Therefore, we can conclude that the influences brought by the Impossible Burger would, in fact, threatened the profits of McDonald at the very start. However, such competition will not cause McDonald always in an adversary position.

One lesson from those sales data is that, it would be good for McDonald to come up with innovations to boost the sales from customers when other competitors provide some new strategies in marketing. But McDonald should also not be too panic since the negative effects brought by competitors will not last for too long. The market will finally balance the trends in customers about chasing for something innovative.

By exploring the average sales from Jan 2016 to Sep 2019, we also find that North East Region contributed to our sales the most. But, overall, all 5 locations were almost the same in contributions to the sales of our products. So, we might put our energies the most in North East region but still pay attentions to the remaining 4 regions.

The bar chart shows that, in those 5 locations, all three products (Hambugers, Chicken Fillet, Fish Fillet) share the same favorite trends: Hambugers are consumed the most, Chicken Fillet the second and Fish Fillet the last. Therefore, I suggest McDonald to invest more on hamburgers since this is the product being loved most by customers.

Also, those data told us that in North East region, all those 3 products are being consumed the most. Therefore, in a quick summary, we should treat North East as the target market for our business. But, since the consumption behaviors are similar in those 5 regions, we could generalize the strategies we will implemented in North East region to the other 4 regions.
